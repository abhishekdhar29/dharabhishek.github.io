Things I am engaged with

Robotics

I have made a dozens of Robots since 3 years. Along with manual robots like Robowar Bot, Task Completing bot,Mars Robber, have also practiced many autonomous bot like Line Following, wall E and many more

Image Processing

Have done couple of projects in Image Processing and Face Detection.Amongst them Automated Attendance system using face detection with openCV Library in Python which is still under process would be a worthy mention.

Web Development

I have made couple of websites for 2 of my clients.I just love designing the front hand of website with HTML, CSS,Bootstrap and back hand designing using php.

Automation

Automation is the worthy solution of Technology Advancement.Now a days to balance the human efforts automation is a must. I have been doing couple of home automation projects a Industrial automation too.

Circuit Design

Being a Electronics and Telecommunication Engineer, designing circuits is not a big deal. I have been passionate in designing analog circuits since time long.

Internet Of things

To make the advancement in technology global, the storage in a server is must. Internet of things is the method of making things connect to the cloud and taking the sensor readings through clouds.I am currently working on a IOT project.
